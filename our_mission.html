Our Mission

Here at Routing.io, we believe that learning about routing algorithms is a crucial aspect of understanding how data is transmitted in computer networks. Our platform aims to simplify the complexities of routing algorithms and make them more accessible to students of all levels, from beginners to advanced learners. By using our tool, students can gain a deeper understanding of how routing algorithms work and how they can be applied in real-world scenarios. Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge and skills they need to become successful network engineers and computer scientists.
